CBG (Clavierhaus Backgammon) brings the real GNU Backgammon engine to a modern touch interface.

The application works entirely offline and contains no advertising, analytics or proprietary online services.

Play

Play complete matches against GNU Backgammon at seven strength levels, from Beginner to Grandmaster. Match play includes the doubling cube, resignations, Crawford, Jacoby, automatic doubles, beavers and selectable match-equity tables.

Train

Play with the Coach enabled and have GNU Backgammon assess each move. The application shows whether a move was best, acceptable or an error, together with stronger alternatives and their equity differences.

Analyse

During play and training, GNU Backgammon evaluates every decision: verdict, equity, rank among the legal plays, and the top alternatives. Match analysis and reviewed playback are built in. (A free-position editor with GNU Backgammon ID and XGID import is in preparation as a separate Plus edition.)

Review

Save matches in GNU Backgammon's SGF format and step through them by game and move. Review the move played, GNU Backgammon's preferred move, the equity cost and the engine's assessment.

GNU Backgammon remains the sole authority for game rules, legal moves, cube decisions and analysis. The Android interface displays engine state and sends explicit user actions to the engine; it does not replace the engine with separate Android-side backgammon logic.

Features include:

* Complete offline operation
* Seven opponent-strength levels
* Full match and doubling-cube play
* Live move assessment
* Position editing and analysis
* GNU Backgammon ID display and copy-out
* SGF match saving and review
* Multiple board themes
* Phone and tablet layouts
* Tap and drag input

Requires Android 12 or newer and a 64-bit ARM device.
